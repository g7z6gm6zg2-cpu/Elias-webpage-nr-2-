Latest Project

PAPER PLANES

What started as a school project took flight and is now on its own journey. "PAPER PLANES" showcases teacher John Dahlquist's young padawans at Bryggeriets Gymnasium, and fresh new talent from the Scandinavian skate scene. The video sets out to shine light on a group of Malmö based skateboarders, showcasing their skills and camaraderie through a poem about flying and landing like a paper plane.

About

Creating from
my surroundings

I'm Elias Egeberg, a young and passionate filmmaker focused on capturing real moments and authentic energy through my camera lens. Inspired by everyday life, movement, and skate culture, I create cinematic visuals that feel honest, raw, and alive.

I'm constantly looking to develop my skills, explore new creative directions, and collaborate with people who share the same passion for visual storytelling.

Whether it's skate videos, brand content, events, or creative projects, I'm always open to new opportunities and challenges.
